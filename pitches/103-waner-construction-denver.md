# Waner Construction Company
## Commercial Construction | Denver, CO

**Industry:** Commercial Construction
**Location:** Littleton, Colorado (Denver metro)
**Estimated Size:** 50-150 employees, employee-owned since 1996
**Website:** wanerconstruction.com

## Pain Points
- Project estimating is time-intensive, requiring manual takeoffs and pricing lookups
- Subcontractor bid management across multiple projects creates administrative burden
- Change order tracking and cost impact analysis is slow and error-prone
- Safety compliance documentation is paper-heavy and audit-stressful

## Proposed AI Solution
- **AI-Assisted Estimating:** Use historical project data to generate faster, more accurate cost estimates with automated material quantity takeoffs
- **Subcontractor Intelligence:** AI-powered bid comparison and subcontractor scoring based on past performance, pricing history, and reliability
- **Change Order Impact Engine:** Instant cost and timeline impact analysis when scope changes hit, with automated client communication drafts
- **Safety Compliance Automation:** Digital safety checklists with AI-flagged anomalies and auto-generated OSHA documentation
