# Park Place Dealerships — Dallas, TX

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Automotive Dealerships (Luxury)
- **Location:** Dallas-Fort Worth, TX
- **Estimated Size:** 2,000-3,000 employees
- **Website:** parkplace.com

## AI Opportunity
**Use Case:** AI-enhanced luxury customer experience and service operations

**Pain Points:**
- Luxury clientele expects white-glove, personalized service at scale — hard to deliver consistently
- Service department scheduling and parts inventory across 16+ locations is complex
- Sales advisors spend hours researching client preferences and vehicle configurations manually

**Proposed Solution:**
- AI client intelligence platform that aggregates purchase history, preferences, and lifestyle data for personalized outreach
- Predictive service scheduling — AI anticipates when each client's vehicle needs service based on mileage and driving patterns
- Parts demand forecasting across all locations to reduce overstock and stockouts
- AI concierge chatbot for after-hours luxury-grade customer support
