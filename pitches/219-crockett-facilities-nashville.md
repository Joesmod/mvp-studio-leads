# Crockett Facilities Services — Nashville, TN

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Professional Services (Facility Management)
- **Location:** Nashville, TN
- **Estimated Size:** 200-500 employees
- **Website:** crockettfacilities.com

## AI Opportunity
**Use Case:** AI-driven facility maintenance scheduling and workforce optimization

**Pain Points:**
- Managing maintenance schedules across dozens of commercial properties is complex and reactive
- High employee turnover in custodial/maintenance roles makes scheduling a constant headache
- Client reporting is manual and time-consuming — pulling together service metrics for each property

**Proposed Solution:**
- Predictive maintenance scheduling based on building usage patterns, equipment age, and historical failure data
- AI workforce scheduler that accounts for skills, certifications, location, and turnover patterns
- Automated client reporting dashboards with AI-generated insights on service quality trends
- IoT sensor integration for real-time building health monitoring (HVAC, plumbing, electrical)
