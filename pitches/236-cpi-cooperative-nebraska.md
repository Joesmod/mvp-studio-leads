# CPI (Cooperative Producers Inc.) — Hastings, NE

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Agriculture / Grain Storage & Handling
- **Location:** Hastings, Nebraska (23 grain facilities across Nebraska)
- **Estimated Size:** 200-500 employees
- **Website:** cpicoop.com

## AI Opportunity
**Grain storage optimization and logistics coordination**

## Pain Points
- Managing 100M+ bushels of storage capacity across 23 facilities
- Coordinating truck and rail logistics during peak harvest season
- Grain quality monitoring across facilities to prevent spoilage
- Commodity price timing decisions for member farmers

## Proposed Solution
AI-powered grain management system that:
- Predicts optimal storage allocation across facilities based on incoming harvest volumes
- Optimizes truck routing and rail car scheduling during peak periods
- Monitors grain condition sensors to predict spoilage risk before it occurs
- Provides market timing signals based on commodity price pattern analysis

## Pitch Angle
"A cooperative managing 100M bushels across 23 facilities — AI logistics optimization during harvest season alone could save millions in transportation costs and prevent spoilage losses."
