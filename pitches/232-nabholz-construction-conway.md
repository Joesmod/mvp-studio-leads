# Nabholz Construction — Conway, AR

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Commercial Construction / Industrial Services
- **Location:** Conway, Arkansas (HQ)
- **Estimated Size:** 1,000-2,000 employees
- **Website:** nabholz.com

## AI Opportunity
**Predictive maintenance for industrial service contracts**

## Pain Points
- Large industrial service division handling HVAC, electrical, and mechanical maintenance
- Reactive maintenance model leads to costly emergency repairs
- Difficulty predicting equipment failures across client facilities
- High labor costs from inefficient dispatch and routing of service technicians

## Proposed Solution
AI-driven predictive maintenance platform that:
- Analyzes equipment sensor data and maintenance history to predict failures
- Optimizes technician routing and dispatch for service calls
- Automates work order generation based on predicted maintenance needs
- Provides dashboards showing equipment health across all client sites

## Pitch Angle
"Nabholz runs both construction and ongoing industrial services — AI predictive maintenance could transform their service division from reactive to proactive, reducing emergency calls by 30-40%."
