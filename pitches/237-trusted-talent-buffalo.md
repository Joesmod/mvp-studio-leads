# Trusted Talent — Buffalo, NY

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Healthcare Staffing
- **Location:** Buffalo, New York
- **Estimated Size:** 50-150 employees (placing hundreds of healthcare workers)
- **Website:** trustedtalent.com

## AI Opportunity
**Staffing match optimization and credential management automation**

## Pain Points
- Matching healthcare workers (nurses, locum tenens) to facility needs is time-intensive
- Credential verification and compliance tracking across multiple states
- High turnover requires constant recruitment pipeline management
- Manual scheduling and shift coverage coordination

## Proposed Solution
AI-powered staffing platform that:
- Matches candidates to open positions based on skills, preferences, location, and facility fit
- Automates credential tracking with expiration alerts and renewal workflows
- Predicts staffing demand based on facility patterns and seasonal trends
- Generates optimized shift schedules that balance worker preferences with facility needs

## Pitch Angle
"Healthcare staffing is a matching problem at scale — AI could reduce time-to-fill by 40% while improving retention by placing workers in better-fit assignments."
