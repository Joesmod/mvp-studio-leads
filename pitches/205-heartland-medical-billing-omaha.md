# Heartland Medical Billing — Omaha, NE

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Healthcare Admin / Medical Billing
- **Location:** Omaha, Nebraska
- **Estimated Size:** 40-60 employees
- **Type:** Third-party medical billing and coding services for clinics

## Pain Points
- High claim denial rate (12-15%) due to coding errors
- Manual chart review for code selection is slow and error-prone
- AR follow-up on denied claims is reactive, not proactive
- Staff turnover means constant retraining

## Proposed AI Solution
AI-assisted medical coding that suggests correct CPT/ICD codes from clinical documentation, reducing errors before submission. Predictive denial management that flags high-risk claims for pre-submission review. Automated AR prioritization that ranks denials by recovery likelihood and dollar value.

## Value Proposition
- Reduce denial rate from 12% to under 5%
- 30% faster coding turnaround with AI assistance
- Recover + annually in currently written-off denials
