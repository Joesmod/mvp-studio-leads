**Industry:** Logistics / LTL Freight
**Location:** Richmond, VA
**Estimated Size:** 22,000+ employees, $4B+ revenue

## Company Overview
Estes Express is one of the largest privately held LTL (less-than-truckload) carriers in the US, operating 280+ terminals nationwide. Family-owned since 1931, they handle millions of shipments annually with a mix of regional and national freight.

## Pain Points
- Route optimization across a massive terminal network
- Dock operations scheduling and load planning
- Claims processing and damage reduction
- Driver retention and recruitment in a tight labor market

## Proposed AI Solution
**AI-Powered Route Optimization & Dock Operations**
- Dynamic route optimization that factors in real-time traffic, weather, and delivery windows
- AI load planning to maximize trailer utilization and minimize handling
- Automated claims processing with image recognition for damage assessment
- Predictive analytics for driver attrition risk and targeted retention programs

## Estimated Impact

- 15% improvement in trailer utilization
- 50% faster claims resolution through automation
- Measurable improvement in driver satisfaction scores
