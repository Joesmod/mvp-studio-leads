## AI Opportunity
**Demand Forecasting & Waste Reduction**

### Pain Points
- Perishable inventory management across 13 stores leads to significant food waste
- Ordering is based on historical averages, not real-time demand signals
- Labor scheduling doesn't adapt well to traffic pattern changes
- Bakery and deli departments have especially high waste rates

### Proposed Solution
AI demand forecasting that incorporates weather, local events, seasonal patterns, and promotion calendars to optimize ordering for each store location. Automated markdown pricing for approaching-expiry items. Smart labor scheduling that adjusts staffing levels based on predicted foot traffic.

### Estimated Impact
- 30% reduction in perishable food waste
- 20% improvement in labor cost efficiency
- 5-8% increase in gross margin through better inventory management
