# Peak Mechanical Services — Salt Lake City, UT

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** HVAC / Mechanical Contracting
- **Location:** Salt Lake City, UT
- **Estimated Size:** 150–250 employees
- **Website:** peakmechanical.com

## Pain Points
- Manual dispatching and scheduling of field technicians across Utah's Wasatch Front
- Paper-based work orders and inspection reports slow down billing cycles
- Seasonal demand spikes (summer cooling, winter heating) cause scheduling chaos
- Difficulty tracking parts inventory across multiple service trucks

## Proposed AI Solution
- **Intelligent dispatch optimization:** AI-powered scheduling that factors in technician skills, location, traffic, and job priority to minimize drive time and maximize daily completions
- **Automated work order processing:** Mobile capture of job details with AI extraction into billing system, cutting invoice turnaround from weeks to days
- **Predictive demand modeling:** Forecast seasonal surges using weather data and historical patterns to pre-position staff and inventory
- **Parts inventory tracking:** Computer vision on truck stock photos to auto-update inventory levels

## Value Proposition
Reduce average dispatch-to-arrival time by 25%, accelerate billing cycles by 60%, and handle 15% more service calls without adding headcount.
