# Gulf Breeze Auto Mall — Mobile, AL

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Auto Dealers
- **Location:** Mobile, Alabama
- **Estimated Size:** 100-150 employees, multi-brand dealership
- **Type:** New and used vehicle sales, service center, parts dept

## Pain Points
- Lead follow-up is inconsistent — sales reps cherry-pick leads
- Service department scheduling done by phone, high no-show rate
- Inventory pricing relies on manager intuition vs market data
- Customer retention outreach is manual and sporadic

## Proposed AI Solution
AI lead scoring and auto-assignment system ensuring every lead gets timely follow-up. Automated service appointment scheduling with SMS reminders reducing no-shows. Dynamic pricing engine that adjusts used car pricing based on market comps, days on lot, and demand signals.

## Value Proposition
- 25% improvement in lead-to-sale conversion with AI scoring
- 40% reduction in service no-shows via automated reminders
- Faster inventory turn with data-driven pricing
