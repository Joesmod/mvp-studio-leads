# Carolina Steel Structures — Raleigh, NC

## Company Info
- **Industry:** Manufacturing (Structural Steel)
- **Location:** Raleigh, NC
- **Estimated Size:** 80–120 employees
- **Type:** Regional structural steel fabricator

## Pain Points
- Manual estimation and quoting for custom steel projects takes days
- Shop floor scheduling relies on whiteboards and spreadsheets
- Quality inspection documentation is paper-based and error-prone
- Material waste tracking is inconsistent

## Proposed AI Solution
- **AI-powered quoting engine** that analyzes project specs and generates estimates in hours instead of days
- **Production scheduling optimization** using ML to balance machine capacity, labor, and deadlines
- **Computer vision QC** — camera-based weld inspection to flag defects before shipment
- **Material usage analytics** to identify waste patterns and optimize cutting plans

## Why Now
Raleigh-Durham construction boom means more demand for steel fabricators. Companies that quote faster win more jobs. AI gives them speed without adding headcount.
