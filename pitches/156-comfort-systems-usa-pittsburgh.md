## AI Use Case: Predictive Maintenance Scheduling & Service Route Optimization

### Pain Points
- Reactive maintenance model leads to emergency callouts and overtime costs
- Service technician routing is manual, wasting drive time between jobs
- Equipment failure history trapped in disconnected work order systems
- Estimating HVAC system replacements is time-consuming and inconsistent

### Proposed Solution
Predictive maintenance engine that analyzes equipment age, service history, and building data to schedule proactive maintenance before failures occur. AI route optimization for service techs that minimizes windshield time. Automated replacement estimating tool based on building specs and equipment catalogs.

### Expected Impact
- 30% reduction in emergency service calls
- 20% improvement in tech utilization (more jobs per day)
- 40% faster replacement estimates
