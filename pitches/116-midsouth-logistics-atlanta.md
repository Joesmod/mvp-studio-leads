# Southeastern Freight Lines — Atlanta, GA

## Company Info
- **Industry:** LTL Freight / Logistics
- **Location:** Lexington, SC (HQ) — major hub in Atlanta, GA
- **Est. Size:** 8,000+ employees
- **Founded:** 1950
- **Type:** Family-owned, regional LTL carrier serving Southeast US

## AI Use Case: Freight Pricing & Dock Operations

### Pain Points
- LTL pricing is complex — thousands of rate combinations based on weight, class, lane, and customer
- Dock loading/unloading sequencing is largely manual, causing trailer dwell time
- Claims processing for damaged freight is slow and paper-heavy
- Driver dispatching doesn't account for real-time traffic or delivery priority optimization

### Proposed Solution
1. **Dynamic Pricing Engine** — AI model that adjusts LTL rates in real-time based on capacity, lane demand, and competitor benchmarking
2. **Smart Dock Scheduler** — Optimize trailer-to-door assignments and load sequencing to reduce dock dwell time
3. **Automated Claims Processor** — AI triage of damage claims with image recognition for damage assessment and auto-routing
4. **Driver Dispatch Optimizer** — Real-time route adjustments factoring in traffic, delivery windows, and driver hours-of-service

### Why Now
LTL carriers face pressure from digital brokerages and tech-enabled competitors. Operational AI is the path to staying competitive.

### Estimated Value
- 5-10% improvement in pricing accuracy (revenue uplift)
- 20% reduction in dock dwell time
- ROI within 5-8 months
