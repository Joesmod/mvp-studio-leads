# Ozark Fleet Management — Springfield, MO

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Logistics / Trucking
- **Location:** Springfield, Missouri
- **Estimated Size:** 150-200 employees, 80+ truck fleet
- **Type:** Regional freight carrier serving Midwest corridor

## Pain Points
- Manual dispatch scheduling via phone/whiteboard
- Paper-based driver logs and compliance tracking
- No real-time visibility into fleet location or ETAs
- High fuel costs with no route optimization

## Proposed AI Solution
AI-powered dispatch and route optimization system that auto-assigns loads based on driver availability, location, and delivery windows. Predictive maintenance alerts based on vehicle telemetry to reduce breakdowns. Automated FMCSA compliance reporting.

## Value Proposition
- 15-20% reduction in empty miles through smart routing
- Automated HOS compliance tracking saves 10+ admin hours/week
- Predictive maintenance reduces roadside breakdowns by 30%
