# Peachtree Property Management — Atlanta, GA

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Real Estate / Property Management
- **Location:** Atlanta, GA
- **Estimated Size:** 50-100 employees
- **Description:** Residential and commercial property management firm handling 2,000+ units across metro Atlanta

## AI Use Case
**Tenant Communication & Maintenance Automation**

## Pain Points
- Maintenance request triage is manual — urgent and routine requests get mixed up
- Tenant communication (leasing inquiries, renewals, complaints) overwhelms small staff
- Vendor coordination for repairs is disorganized

## Proposed Solution
AI-powered tenant portal that auto-triages maintenance requests by urgency, routes to appropriate vendors, and provides real-time status updates. Conversational AI for leasing inquiries and renewal processing. Predictive maintenance scheduling based on unit age and history. Target: 60% reduction in admin overhead, 24/7 tenant response.
