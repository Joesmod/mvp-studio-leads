## AI Use Case: Customer Lifecycle Management & Service Scheduling
Park Place operates luxury dealerships (Mercedes, Lexus, Porsche, etc.) where customer experience is everything. AI can personalize the entire ownership journey.

## Pain Points
- Manual follow-up processes for service reminders and trade-in timing
- Inventory allocation across 16 locations based on gut feel
- Service department scheduling inefficiencies and long wait times
- Difficulty predicting which customers are ready to buy or trade up

## Proposed Solution
- AI-driven customer propensity scoring for trade-in and purchase timing
- Predictive service scheduling based on vehicle telemetry and mileage patterns
- Inventory optimization across locations using local demand signals
- Automated personalized outreach via email/SMS based on ownership lifecycle stage

## Estimated Impact
- 15-20% increase in service appointment utilization
- Higher trade-in capture rate through timely outreach
- Better inventory turns by matching stock to local demand
