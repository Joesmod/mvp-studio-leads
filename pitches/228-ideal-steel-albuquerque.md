# Ideal Steel Supply — Albuquerque, NM

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Manufacturing / Steel Distribution
- **Location:** Albuquerque, NM
- **Estimated Size:** 50-150 employees
- **Website:** idealsteelsupply.com

## AI Opportunity
**Inventory Optimization & Quote Automation**

### Pain Points
- Steel distribution requires managing thousands of SKUs across grades, sizes, and finishes
- Quoting is manual and slow — customers wait hours or days for pricing on custom orders
- Demand is cyclical and tied to regional construction activity that's hard to predict
- Cut-to-order operations waste material without optimal nesting patterns

### Proposed Solution
AI-powered quoting engine that generates instant pricing based on material costs, cutting complexity, and current inventory levels. Demand forecasting tied to regional construction permits and economic indicators. Automated nesting optimization for cut-to-order operations to minimize material waste.

### Estimated Impact
- Quote turnaround from hours to seconds
- 10-15% reduction in material waste through optimized cutting
- Better inventory positioning reducing stockouts by 25%
