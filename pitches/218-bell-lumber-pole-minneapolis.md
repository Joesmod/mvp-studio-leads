# Bell Lumber & Pole — Minneapolis, MN

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Manufacturing (Wood Products/Utility Poles)
- **Location:** New Brighton, MN (Minneapolis metro)
- **Estimated Size:** 100-300 employees
- **Website:** blpole.com

## AI Opportunity
**Use Case:** AI quality grading and supply chain optimization

**Pain Points:**
- Utility pole grading is a manual, skilled-labor-intensive process with inconsistent results
- Timber sourcing depends on seasonal availability and fluctuating lumber markets
- Logistics coordination for oversized loads (60-120ft poles) requires specialized planning

**Proposed Solution:**
- Computer vision system for automated pole grading — detect knots, splits, and decay faster than human inspectors
- AI-powered timber market forecasting to optimize purchasing decisions
- Specialized logistics AI for oversized load routing, permit management, and delivery scheduling
- Predictive demand modeling based on utility company infrastructure plans and storm damage patterns
