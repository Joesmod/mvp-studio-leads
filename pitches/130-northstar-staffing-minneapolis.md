# NorthStar Staffing Solutions — Minneapolis, MN

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Professional Services / Staffing
- **Location:** Minneapolis, Minnesota
- **Estimated Size:** 60-120 internal employees, places 2,000+ workers annually
- **Type:** Light industrial and administrative staffing agency

## Pain Points
- Resume screening is manual — recruiters spend 70% of time reviewing unqualified applicants
- Client job orders come in via email/phone with inconsistent formatting; manual data entry into ATS
- Worker no-shows are a constant problem — 15-20% no-show rate on first-day assignments
- Compliance tracking (I-9, certifications, drug tests) is spreadsheet-based and error-prone

## Proposed AI Solution
- **AI resume screening & matching** — instantly score and rank applicants against job requirements, surface top candidates
- **Automated job order intake** — AI parses client emails/calls into structured ATS entries, confirms details via auto-reply
- **No-show prediction & prevention** — ML model flags high-risk placements; triggers extra confirmation touchpoints
- **Compliance automation** — AI tracks document expirations, sends renewal reminders, blocks non-compliant placements

## Estimated ROI
- Recruiter productivity: 3x more placements per recruiter
- No-show rate reduction: from 18% to 8%
- Compliance violations: near-zero
- Time-to-fill reduction: 40%

## Contact Strategy
- Target: Branch Manager or VP of Operations
- Angle: "Your recruiters are drowning in resumes while your best candidates get hired elsewhere. AI fixes that in weeks, not months"
