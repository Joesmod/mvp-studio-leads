# Sunbelt Property Services — San Antonio, TX

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Real Estate / Property Management
- **Location:** San Antonio, Texas
- **Estimated Size:** 50-70 employees, managing 3,000+ units
- **Type:** Residential property management company

## Pain Points
- Maintenance requests handled via phone/email, no prioritization
- Tenant screening is slow — manual background/credit checks
- Lease renewals tracked in spreadsheets, renewals often missed
- Vendor coordination for repairs is phone tag and lost invoices

## Proposed AI Solution
AI maintenance triage system that auto-categorizes and prioritizes work orders, dispatches vendors, and provides tenants with status updates. Automated tenant screening pipeline with AI risk scoring. Smart lease management with automated renewal reminders and rent adjustment recommendations based on market comps.

## Value Proposition
- 50% faster maintenance response time with auto-dispatch
- Reduce vacancy loss with proactive lease renewal management
- Save 20+ hours/week on admin tasks across the portfolio
