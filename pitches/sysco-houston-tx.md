## AI Use Case: Warehouse Automation & Demand Forecasting
Sysco is the world's largest foodservice distributor, operating 330+ distribution facilities. Managing perishable inventory at that scale is a massive coordination challenge.

## Pain Points
- Perishable inventory spoilage across hundreds of warehouses
- Manual order picking and routing for thousands of daily deliveries
- Seasonal and event-driven demand spikes difficult to predict
- Driver scheduling and last-mile delivery optimization

## Proposed Solution
- AI-powered demand forecasting factoring in seasonality, local events, and weather
- Computer vision for automated quality inspection of perishable goods at receiving
- Dynamic route optimization for delivery fleets reducing miles and fuel
- Intelligent warehouse slotting to reduce pick times

## Estimated Impact
- 15-25% reduction in perishable waste
- 10-20% improvement in delivery route efficiency
- Faster order fulfillment through optimized warehouse layout
