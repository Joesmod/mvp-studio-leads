# Roaster Joe — Wichita, KS

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Food & Beverage Manufacturing (Coffee Roasting)
- **Location:** Wichita, Kansas
- **Estimated Size:** 51-200 employees
- **Revenue:** ~$12M
- **Website:** roasterjoe.com

## AI Opportunity
**Production quality control and demand forecasting**

## Pain Points
- Coffee roasting requires precise temperature and timing control for consistency
- Seasonal demand fluctuations make production planning difficult
- Waste from over-roasting or quality inconsistencies
- Manual quality grading process is slow and subjective

## Proposed Solution
AI-driven production optimization that:
- Monitors roasting parameters in real-time and predicts optimal roast profiles
- Forecasts demand by SKU based on seasonal patterns, wholesale orders, and trends
- Computer vision quality grading to replace manual bean inspection
- Automated inventory management for green bean sourcing and storage

## Pitch Angle
"A $12M specialty coffee roaster — AI quality control and demand forecasting could reduce waste by 20% while ensuring consistent product quality across every batch."
