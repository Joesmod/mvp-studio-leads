## AI Use Case: Lease Management & Tenant Experience Automation

## Pain Points
- Lease abstraction and renewal tracking spread across spreadsheets
- Tenant maintenance requests handled through email/phone with no prioritization

## Proposed Solution
- AI-powered lease abstraction extracting key terms, dates, and clauses automatically
- Intelligent maintenance ticketing with auto-categorization and priority routing
- Tenant churn prediction based on payment patterns, request frequency, and market conditions
- Building energy optimization using occupancy patterns and weather data

- 70% faster lease abstraction and review

- 10-15% energy cost reduction across managed portfolio
