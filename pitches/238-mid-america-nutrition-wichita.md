# Mid America Nutrition Programs — Wichita, KS

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Food Manufacturing / Cereal & Nutrition Products
- **Location:** Wichita, Kansas
- **Estimated Size:** 50-200 employees
- **Website:** cerealfood.com

## AI Opportunity
**Production line efficiency and nutritional compliance automation**

## Pain Points
- Nutrition product manufacturing requires strict regulatory compliance (FDA labeling)
- Production line changeovers between product types create downtime
- Quality control sampling is manual and time-consuming
- Ingredient sourcing and cost optimization across volatile commodity markets

## Proposed Solution
AI-driven manufacturing optimization that:
- Automates nutritional label generation and FDA compliance checking
- Optimizes production line scheduling to minimize changeover downtime
- Predicts ingredient cost trends for better procurement timing
- Computer vision QC to catch defects on the production line in real-time

## Pitch Angle
"A specialized nutrition manufacturer in Wichita — AI compliance automation alone could save hundreds of hours annually on FDA labeling, while production optimization reduces costly line changeovers."
