## AI Opportunity
**Route Optimization & Driver Retention Analytics**

### Pain Points
- Flatbed trucking has unique load planning challenges (oversized, irregular freight)
- Driver turnover is a major cost center in the trucking industry (100%+ annual rates)
- Fuel costs are a top expense with limited optimization beyond basic routing
- Load matching and capacity planning rely on dispatcher experience

### Proposed Solution
AI route optimization tailored for flatbed operations, factoring in load dimensions, bridge clearances, weather, and fuel prices. Driver retention prediction model that identifies at-risk drivers based on behavioral signals (route preferences, home time patterns, pay satisfaction) and triggers proactive retention interventions. Automated load-matching to maximize truck utilization.

### Estimated Impact
- 8-12% fuel cost reduction through optimized routing
- 20% improvement in driver retention through early intervention
- 15% increase in revenue per truck through better load matching
