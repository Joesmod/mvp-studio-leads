# Tidewater Logistics — Norfolk, VA

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Logistics / Freight
- **Location:** Norfolk, VA
- **Estimated Size:** 80-150 employees
- **Description:** Regional freight brokerage and warehousing operation leveraging proximity to the Port of Virginia for East Coast distribution

## AI Use Case
**AI Freight Matching & Warehouse Slotting**

## Pain Points
- Brokers manually match loads to carriers via phone and load boards, leaving money on the table from suboptimal pricing
- Warehouse slotting is static — high-velocity SKUs buried behind slow movers increases pick times
- Dwell time at port and warehouse docks is unpredictable, creating costly detention charges

## Proposed Solution
AI freight matching engine that recommends optimal carriers based on lane history, pricing trends, and reliability scores. Dynamic warehouse slotting AI that reorganizes pick locations based on order patterns. Predictive dock scheduling to minimize dwell and detention. Target: 10% freight cost reduction, 20% faster warehouse picks.
