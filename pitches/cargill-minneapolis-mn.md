## AI Use Case: Supply Chain Optimization & Crop Yield Prediction
Cargill's agricultural supply chain spans global sourcing, processing, and distribution. Even small efficiency gains at their scale translate to hundreds of millions.

## Pain Points
- Crop quality variability affecting processing yields
- Complex global supply chain with weather/geopolitical disruption risk
- Manual grain grading and quality assessment at receiving
- Energy consumption optimization across processing facilities

## Proposed Solution
- Computer vision for automated grain grading and quality classification at intake
- AI-driven supply chain risk modeling incorporating weather, trade policy, and logistics data
- Process optimization models for feed mills and processing plants to reduce energy waste
- Yield prediction models helping procurement teams optimize forward contracts

## Estimated Impact
- Faster, more consistent quality grading at scale
- Early warning on supply chain disruptions enabling proactive sourcing
- 5-10% energy reduction in processing facilities
