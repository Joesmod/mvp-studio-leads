# Motor City Auto Group — Detroit, MI

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Auto Dealerships
- **Location:** Detroit, MI
- **Estimated Size:** 150-300 employees
- **Description:** Multi-brand auto dealer group with 5+ locations across metro Detroit, handling new/used sales, service, and parts

## AI Use Case
**AI Service Department Optimization & Lead Scoring**

## Pain Points
- Service advisors manually estimate repair times, leading to overbooking and long wait times
- Internet leads sit in CRM for hours before follow-up, losing hot buyers to competitors
- Parts inventory across locations is siloed — one store overstocks while another runs out

## Proposed Solution
AI service scheduler that predicts repair duration based on vehicle history, technician skill level, and parts availability. AI lead scoring and auto-response system that engages internet leads within minutes with personalized messaging. Cross-location parts inventory optimizer. Target: 40% faster lead response, 20% improvement in service bay utilization.
