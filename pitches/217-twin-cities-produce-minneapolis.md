# Bix Produce Company — Minneapolis, MN

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Food & Beverage Distribution (Produce)
- **Location:** Minneapolis, MN
- **Estimated Size:** 200-400 employees
- **Website:** bixproduce.com

## AI Opportunity
**Use Case:** AI-powered freshness tracking and order optimization

**Pain Points:**
- Produce spoilage is a constant margin killer — shelf life varies by item and season
- Manual order-taking from restaurants is time-intensive and error-prone
- Pricing fluctuates daily based on market conditions — reps struggle to stay current

**Proposed Solution:**
- AI freshness and shelf-life prediction system to optimize inventory rotation and reduce waste
- Digital ordering platform with AI-suggested orders based on each restaurant's historical patterns
- Dynamic pricing engine that adjusts quotes based on real-time market data and supply levels
- Route optimization for temperature-sensitive deliveries across the Twin Cities metro
