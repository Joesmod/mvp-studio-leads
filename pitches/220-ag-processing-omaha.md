# Ag Processing Inc (AGP) — Omaha, NE

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Agriculture (Soybean Processing)
- **Location:** Omaha, NE
- **Estimated Size:** 1,000-2,000 employees
- **Website:** agp.com

## AI Opportunity
**Use Case:** AI-optimized processing operations and grain procurement

**Pain Points:**
- Soybean processing yield varies with input quality — currently assessed manually
- Grain procurement decisions rely on trader experience rather than data-driven models
- Energy costs across multiple processing plants are significant and hard to optimize

**Proposed Solution:**
- AI quality assessment at intake — predict processing yield based on moisture, protein, and oil content analysis
- Market intelligence AI for grain procurement — optimal timing and sourcing recommendations
- Energy optimization across processing plants using AI to adjust operations based on utility rates and production schedules
- Predictive maintenance for processing equipment to minimize costly unplanned shutdowns
