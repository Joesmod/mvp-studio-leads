# Great Lakes Stamping — Detroit, MI

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Manufacturing / Metal Stamping
- **Location:** Detroit, MI
- **Estimated Size:** 100-250 employees
- **Description:** Precision metal stamping and tooling company serving automotive and industrial OEMs

## AI Use Case
**Visual Quality Inspection & Die Life Prediction**

## Pain Points
- Manual visual inspection of stamped parts misses defects, causing costly returns
- Die wear tracking is reactive — dies fail mid-run causing scrap and downtime
- Production scheduling across multiple presses is suboptimal

## Proposed Solution
Computer vision defect detection on stamping lines for real-time quality control. Predictive die life modeling based on material, run counts, and sensor data. AI production scheduler optimizing press utilization and changeover sequences. Target: 80% fewer escaped defects, 35% less unplanned die downtime.
