# Southeast Mechanical Services — Atlanta, GA

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Commercial HVAC / Mechanical Contracting
- **Location:** Atlanta, Georgia
- **Estimated Size:** 100-200 employees
- **Type:** Commercial HVAC installation, maintenance, and service

## Pain Points
- Dispatching technicians is reactive — no optimization for geography, skill match, or parts availability
- Estimating/bidding on new projects takes 2-3 weeks; losing jobs to faster competitors
- Preventive maintenance contracts tracked in spreadsheets — missed visits erode client trust
- Technicians spend 30% of time on paperwork (work orders, compliance docs, photos)

## Proposed AI Solution
- **Intelligent dispatch** — AI assigns techs based on location, certifications, parts on truck, and real-time traffic
- **Automated estimating** — AI pre-builds estimates from blueprints/specs using historical project data, cut bid time by 70%
- **PM contract automation** — auto-schedule visits, generate checklists, alert on overdue maintenance
- **Mobile AI assistant for techs** — voice-to-text work orders, auto-tag photos, generate compliance reports on-site

## Estimated ROI
- 25% more jobs completed per tech per day
- Bid turnaround: from 2-3 weeks to 3-5 days
- PM contract retention improvement: 20%

## Contact Strategy
- Target: VP of Operations or Service Manager
- Angle: "Your techs are your biggest asset — AI handles the admin so they can handle more calls"
