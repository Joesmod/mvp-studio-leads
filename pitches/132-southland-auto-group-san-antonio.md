# Southland Auto Group — San Antonio, TX

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Automotive Dealerships
- **Location:** San Antonio, TX
- **Estimated Size:** 200–400 employees (multi-location)
- **Website:** southlandautogroup.com

## Pain Points
- Sales team spends hours manually following up on internet leads — many go cold
- Service department scheduling is phone-based, leading to long hold times and no-shows
- Used car reconditioning turnaround is slow with no visibility into bottleneck stages
- Customer satisfaction surveys are reviewed manually weeks after visit

## Proposed AI Solution
- **AI lead response system:** Instant, personalized follow-up to web/email inquiries within minutes, qualifying buyers and booking test drives automatically
- **Service appointment AI assistant:** Conversational booking via text/web that handles scheduling, reminders, and upsell recommendations based on vehicle history
- **Reconditioning workflow tracker:** AI-powered stage tracking from acquisition to lot-ready, identifying bottlenecks and predicting completion dates
- **Real-time sentiment analysis:** Auto-process survey and review data to flag issues immediately for management action

## Value Proposition
Convert 30% more internet leads, reduce service no-shows by 40%, and cut reconditioning cycle time by 2 days.
