# Desert Ridge Property Management — Phoenix, AZ

## Company Info
- **Industry:** Real Estate (Property Management)
- **Location:** Phoenix, AZ
- **Estimated Size:** 40–70 employees
- **Type:** Residential property management, 3,000+ units

## Pain Points
- Maintenance request triage is manual — emergency vs routine takes staff time to sort
- Tenant screening is slow, averaging 3-5 days per application
- Lease renewal outreach is reactive, not proactive — losing tenants to competitors
- Vendor invoice reconciliation across 3,000 units is a spreadsheet nightmare

## Proposed AI Solution
- **AI maintenance triage** — auto-categorizes requests by urgency, assigns to correct vendor, and provides tenants with instant troubleshooting steps
- **Instant tenant screening** — AI pulls and analyzes credit, criminal, rental history in under 1 hour
- **Predictive retention** — identifies tenants likely to leave 90 days before lease end, triggers personalized renewal offers
- **Automated AP processing** — AI matches invoices to work orders, flags discrepancies, and auto-codes expenses

## Why Now
Phoenix rental market is booming but competitive. Property managers who respond faster and retain better win owner contracts.
