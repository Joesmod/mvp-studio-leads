# Ken Garff Automotive Group — Salt Lake City, UT

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Auto Dealerships
- **Location:** Salt Lake City, Utah (HQ), 60+ dealerships across western US
- **Estimated Size:** 5,000+ employees
- **Website:** kengarff.com

## AI Opportunity
**Customer lifecycle management and inventory optimization**

## Pain Points
- Managing inventory across 60+ dealerships with varying regional demand
- Customer follow-up and lead nurturing at scale across multiple brands
- Service department scheduling inefficiencies
- Pricing optimization across new and used vehicle inventory

## Proposed Solution
AI-powered dealership operations platform that:
- Predicts optimal inventory mix per location based on regional demand patterns
- Automates personalized customer follow-up sequences based on buying signals
- Optimizes service appointment scheduling to maximize bay utilization
- Dynamic pricing recommendations for used vehicles based on market conditions

## Pitch Angle
"With 60+ dealerships and 20+ brands, Ken Garff has massive data but likely fragmented systems — AI could unify insights across locations and drive 15-20% improvement in inventory turn rates."
