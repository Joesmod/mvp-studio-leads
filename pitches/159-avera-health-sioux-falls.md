## AI Use Case: Patient Scheduling Optimization & Revenue Cycle Automation

### Pain Points
- Patient no-show rates high across rural clinic network (15-20%)
- Prior authorization processing is manual and delays patient care
- Coding and billing errors lead to claim denials and revenue leakage
- Staff scheduling across 30+ rural facilities is complex and reactive

### Proposed Solution
AI no-show prediction model that triggers automated reminders and enables overbooking optimization. NLP-powered prior authorization system that extracts clinical data from notes and auto-populates payer forms. Automated coding review that flags likely denials before submission. Demand-based staff scheduling across the rural network.

### Expected Impact
- 30% reduction in no-show rates
- 50% faster prior authorization turnaround
- 15% reduction in claim denial rate
