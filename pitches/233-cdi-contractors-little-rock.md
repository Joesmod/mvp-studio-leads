# CDI Contractors — Little Rock, AR

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** General Contracting / Construction Management
- **Location:** Little Rock, AR (also Fayetteville, AR and Frisco, TX)
- **Estimated Size:** 200-500 employees
- **Website:** cdicon.com

## AI Opportunity
**Bid estimation and document processing automation**

## Pain Points
- Complex bid preparation for high-profile commercial projects
- Manual review of hundreds of pages of construction documents per project
- Estimating errors that lead to underbidding or losing competitive bids
- Multi-office coordination across three states

## Proposed Solution
AI-powered bid intelligence system that:
- Extracts key requirements from RFPs and construction documents automatically
- Compares historical bid data to improve cost estimation accuracy
- Identifies risk factors and scope gaps in project specifications
- Generates bid summaries and comparison reports across competing opportunities

## Pitch Angle
"CDI builds high-profile, one-of-a-kind structures — AI bid analysis could cut estimation time by 50% while improving accuracy, directly impacting their win rate on competitive projects."
