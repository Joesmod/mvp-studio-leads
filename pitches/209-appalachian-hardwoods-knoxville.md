# Appalachian Hardwoods — Knoxville, TN

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Manufacturing / Lumber Processing
- **Location:** Knoxville, Tennessee
- **Estimated Size:** 90-130 employees
- **Type:** Hardwood lumber processing and distribution

## Pain Points
- Lumber grading is 100% manual — experienced graders are retiring
- Sawmill yield optimization relies on operator experience
- Inventory tracking across drying kilns and warehouse is paper-based
- Customer order fulfillment is slow due to manual inventory matching

## Proposed AI Solution
Computer vision lumber grading system that assists graders with consistent, fast grade classification. AI sawmill optimization that recommends cutting patterns to maximize board footage yield. Digital inventory system with real-time tracking across kiln, warehouse, and shipping stages.

## Value Proposition
- Maintain grading consistency as experienced workforce retires
- 5-8% improvement in lumber yield through optimized cutting
- Real-time inventory visibility cuts order fulfillment time by 40%
