# Suncoast Medical Billing Services — Tampa, FL

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Healthcare Administration / Medical Billing
- **Location:** Tampa, FL
- **Estimated Size:** 80–150 employees
- **Clients:** 200+ physician practices across Florida

## Pain Points
- Claim denials run at 12-15% — staff manually reviews and resubmits, losing revenue
- Coding accuracy depends on individual coder experience; inconsistency across team
- Patient eligibility verification is a manual phone/portal process before every visit
- AR aging reports reviewed weekly — delinquent accounts not caught fast enough

## Proposed AI Solution
- **Denial prediction and prevention:** AI analyzes claim data to flag likely denials before submission, suggesting corrections to reduce rejection rate
- **AI coding assistant:** Reviews clinical documentation and suggests appropriate codes, improving consistency and reducing audit risk
- **Automated eligibility verification:** AI checks eligibility across payer portals in batch, flagging issues before appointments
- **Intelligent AR management:** Daily AI analysis of aging accounts, prioritizing follow-up by recovery likelihood and dollar value

## Value Proposition
Reduce denial rate from 12% to under 5%, accelerate collections by 20 days, and free up 30% of staff time from manual verification.
