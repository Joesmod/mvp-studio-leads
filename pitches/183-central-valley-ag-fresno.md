## AI Use Case
**AI Irrigation & Crop Health Monitoring**

## Pain Points
- Irrigation scheduling relies on farmer intuition rather than soil/weather data, wasting water in a drought-prone region
- Pest and disease detection happens after visible damage, reducing yields
- Seasonal labor coordination is chaotic with no demand forecasting

## Proposed Solution
AI irrigation controller integrating soil moisture sensors, weather forecasts, and crop water-need models to optimize water use. Drone/satellite imagery with AI analysis for early pest and disease detection. Predictive labor demand model based on crop maturity and harvest windows. Target: 25% water savings, 15% yield improvement from early intervention.
