## AI Opportunity
**Claims Processing & Patient Flow Optimization**

### Pain Points
- High volume of insurance claims with significant denial rates
- Patient scheduling across multiple facilities leads to bottlenecks and long wait times
- Clinical documentation is time-consuming for providers, reducing patient face time
- Staffing optimization across departments is reactive rather than predictive

### Proposed Solution
AI claims scrubbing that catches errors before submission, reducing denial rates. Predictive patient flow modeling that optimizes scheduling across facilities and departments. AI-assisted clinical documentation that drafts notes from visit recordings. Predictive staffing model based on patient volume forecasts.

### Estimated Impact
- 40% reduction in claims denials
- 25% improvement in patient throughput
- 2+ hours per day saved for providers on documentation
