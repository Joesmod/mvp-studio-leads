# Covenant Logistics
## Trucking & Freight | Chattanooga, TN

**Industry:** Logistics / Trucking
**Estimated Size:** 6,000+ employees
**Website:** covenantlogistics.com

### Pain Points
- Driver retention — high turnover costs millions annually in recruiting and training
- Load matching and capacity optimization still involves manual broker calls
- Fuel cost management across thousands of trucks

### Proposed AI Solution
AI **driver retention predictor** — analyzes patterns (route difficulty, home time, pay progression) to flag at-risk drivers before they quit, enabling proactive retention offers. Plus AI load optimization that maximizes revenue per mile.

### Why Now
Driver shortage is the #1 industry crisis. Retaining existing drivers via AI insights is cheaper than recruiting new ones.
