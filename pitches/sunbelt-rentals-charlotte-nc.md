# Sunbelt Rentals — Charlotte, NC

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Equipment Rental / Construction Services
- **Location:** Charlotte, North Carolina
- **Estimated Size:** 17,000+ employees, 1,100+ locations, $7B+ revenue
- **Website:** sunbeltrentals.com

## AI Use Case: Fleet Utilization & Predictive Maintenance
Sunbelt manages hundreds of thousands of rental assets — from scissor lifts to generators. Maximizing utilization while minimizing downtime is the core business challenge.

## Pain Points
- Underutilized equipment sitting idle at wrong locations
- Reactive maintenance causing unexpected rental failures and customer dissatisfaction
- Manual pricing decisions not reflecting real-time local demand
- Logistics coordination for equipment delivery and pickup across 1,100 branches

## Proposed Solution
- AI-driven fleet rebalancing recommendations based on regional demand forecasting
- Predictive maintenance using equipment telemetry and usage patterns
- Dynamic pricing engine adjusting rental rates based on local utilization and demand
- Route optimization for delivery/pickup logistics

## Estimated Impact
- 10-15% improvement in fleet utilization rates
- 25% reduction in unplanned maintenance events
- Revenue uplift through demand-responsive pricing
