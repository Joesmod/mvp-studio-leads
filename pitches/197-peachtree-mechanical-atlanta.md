# Peachtree Mechanical Contractors — Atlanta, GA

## Company Info
- **Industry:** Construction (Mechanical/HVAC)
- **Location:** Atlanta, GA
- **Estimated Size:** 120–200 employees
- **Type:** Commercial HVAC and plumbing contractor

## Pain Points
- Estimating bids takes 2-3 weeks for complex commercial projects
- Field technician dispatching is reactive, not optimized for geography or skill
- Change order documentation is inconsistent, leading to revenue leakage
- Apprentice training and knowledge transfer from retiring journeymen is ad-hoc

## Proposed AI Solution
- **AI-assisted estimating** — analyzes historical bids, material costs, and project specs to generate estimates 60% faster
- **Smart dispatch** — optimizes tech routing by location, skillset, parts on truck, and job priority
- **Automated change order tracking** — AI flags scope changes in real-time and generates documentation
- **Knowledge capture system** — AI records and indexes experienced tech troubleshooting methods for training

## Why Now
Atlanta commercial construction is strong but labor is tight. AI helps do more with fewer people and protects institutional knowledge.
