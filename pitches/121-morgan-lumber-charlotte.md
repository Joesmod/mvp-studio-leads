# Morgan Lumber Company — Charlotte, NC

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Construction / Building Materials
- **Location:** Charlotte, North Carolina
- **Estimated Size:** 80-150 employees
- **Type:** Regional lumber and building materials distributor

## Pain Points
- Manual inventory tracking across multiple warehouse locations
- Lumber pricing fluctuates daily; sales team struggles to quote accurately in real-time
- Delivery scheduling done via phone/whiteboard, leading to missed windows and idle trucks
- Customer reorders are manual — no predictive system for recurring buyers

## Proposed AI Solution
- **Demand forecasting engine** that predicts material needs by season, housing starts data, and historical orders
- **Dynamic pricing assistant** that adjusts quotes based on commodity market feeds
- **Route optimization** for delivery fleet — reduce fuel costs 15-20%
- **Automated reorder alerts** for top accounts based on purchase cycle patterns

## Estimated ROI
- 12-18% reduction in dead stock
- 20% faster quote turnaround
- Delivery cost savings of $8-15K/month

## Contact Strategy
- Target: Operations Manager or VP of Sales
- Angle: "Your competitors are using AI to quote faster and deliver smarter — let's make sure you're not left behind"
