## AI Use Case: Inventory Pricing & Lead Scoring

### Pain Points
- Manual pricing decisions on used vehicle inventory across multiple lots
- Sales team spends hours qualifying cold leads from web inquiries
- Service department scheduling is reactive, leading to bay downtime
- No unified view of customer lifecycle across sales and service

### Proposed Solution
AI-powered dynamic pricing tool that adjusts used car list prices based on market comps, days-on-lot, and seasonal demand. Automated lead scoring from website/CRM data to prioritize high-intent buyers. Predictive service scheduling to fill bay capacity gaps.

### Expected Impact
- 10-15% improvement in used vehicle turn rate
- 25% increase in sales team efficiency via lead prioritization
- 20% reduction in service bay idle time
