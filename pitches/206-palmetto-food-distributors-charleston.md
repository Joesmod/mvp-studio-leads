# Palmetto Food Distributors — Charleston, SC

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Food & Beverage / Distribution
- **Location:** Charleston, South Carolina
- **Estimated Size:** 120-180 employees
- **Type:** Regional food distributor serving restaurants and institutions

## Pain Points
- Demand forecasting done manually, leading to spoilage and stockouts
- Route planning for delivery trucks is static and inefficient
- Order entry still partially fax/phone-based from older restaurant clients
- Warehouse picking errors cause returns and customer complaints

## Proposed AI Solution
AI demand forecasting engine that predicts order volumes by customer, product, and season to optimize purchasing. Dynamic delivery route optimization reducing fuel and driver hours. AI-powered voice ordering system that lets customers call in orders processed automatically.

## Value Proposition
- Reduce food spoilage by 20% with better demand prediction
- Cut delivery costs 15% with optimized routing
- Eliminate manual order entry errors with AI voice processing
