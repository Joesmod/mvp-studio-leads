# Colonial Pest Control — Boston, MA

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Professional Services / Pest Management
- **Location:** Boston, MA
- **Estimated Size:** 75–125 employees
- **Service Area:** Greater New England

## Pain Points
- Technician routing is inefficient — dispatchers manually assign daily routes across wide service area
- Service reports handwritten in the field, then re-entered into system at end of day
- Customer call center overwhelmed during pest season (spring/summer) — long hold times
- Recurring service contracts tracked in spreadsheets; missed renewals lose revenue

## Proposed AI Solution
- **AI route optimization:** Dynamic daily routing that factors in appointment windows, traffic, technician certifications, and equipment on truck
- **Mobile AI reporting:** Voice-to-text field reports with AI-structured data entry — findings, treatments applied, and follow-up recommendations auto-populated
- **AI customer service agent:** Handle inbound calls for scheduling, billing questions, and service inquiries — escalate complex issues to humans
- **Contract lifecycle management:** AI tracks renewal dates, usage patterns, and customer health scores to proactively retain and upsell accounts

## Value Proposition
Increase daily service calls per tech by 20%, eliminate data entry backlog, and reduce customer churn by 15%.
