# Goodman Manufacturing
## AI Integration Consulting Pitch

**Industry:** HVAC Manufacturing
**Location:** Houston, TX
**Estimated Size:** 5,000+ employees (Daikin subsidiary but operates independently)
**Website:** goodmanmfg.com

## Company Overview
Goodman Manufacturing is one of the largest residential HVAC manufacturers in North America, producing air conditioners, furnaces, heat pumps, and air handlers. Their Houston campus is one of the largest HVAC production facilities in the world.

## Pain Points
- Warranty claims processing — high volume, complex diagnosis codes
- Dealer network support — thousands of contractors need technical assistance
- Production quality variance detection across high-speed assembly lines
- Supply chain planning for seasonal demand (summer AC, winter heating)

## Proposed AI Solution
**AI-Powered Warranty Analytics & Dealer Support**
- Automated warranty claims triage using NLP to classify and route claims faster
- AI technical support chatbot for dealer network — instant answers from service manuals and bulletins
- Computer vision on assembly lines for quality defect detection
- Seasonal demand forecasting integrated with supplier lead times

## Estimated Impact
- 40-50% faster warranty claims resolution
- 30% reduction in dealer support call volume through AI self-service
- Early detection of quality trends before they become field failures
- Better seasonal inventory positioning reducing backorders

## Outreach Strategy
Target VP of Customer Support or Director of Quality. Warranty cost reduction is a massive lever for HVAC manufacturers — every percentage point saved drops straight to margin.
