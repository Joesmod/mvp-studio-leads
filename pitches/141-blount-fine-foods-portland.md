# Blount Fine Foods
## AI Integration Consulting Pitch

**Industry:** Food Manufacturing
**Location:** Portland, OR (Fall River, MA HQ — Portland distribution)
**Estimated Size:** 1,000+ employees, $500M+ revenue
**Website:** blountfinefoods.com

## Company Overview
Blount Fine Foods is a major manufacturer of premium soups, side dishes, and meal solutions for retail and foodservice. They operate large-scale production facilities with complex supply chains across fresh and frozen categories.

## Pain Points
- Demand forecasting for perishable products with short shelf life
- Production scheduling across multiple SKUs with seasonal variation
- Quality control consistency across high-volume batch processing
- Ingredient waste reduction in fresh food manufacturing

## Proposed AI Solution
**AI-Powered Demand Forecasting & Production Optimization**
- Machine learning demand forecasting using POS data, weather, seasonality, and promotional calendars
- Automated production scheduling that minimizes changeover time and ingredient waste
- Computer vision quality inspection on packaging lines
- Predictive maintenance for processing equipment (kettles, fillers, packaging lines)

## Estimated Impact
- 15-25% reduction in food waste through better demand matching
- 10-20% improvement in production line utilization
- Faster response to demand shifts (retail vs foodservice channels)

## Outreach Strategy
Target VP of Operations or Director of Supply Chain. Lead with waste reduction ROI — in fresh food manufacturing, even small improvements in forecasting translate to significant savings.
