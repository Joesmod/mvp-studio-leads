# Palermo's Pizza
## AI Integration Consulting Pitch

**Industry:** Food Manufacturing
**Location:** Milwaukee, WI
**Estimated Size:** 600+ employees, $200M+ revenue
**Website:** palermospizza.com

## Company Overview
Palermo's is one of the largest frozen pizza manufacturers in the US, producing private-label and branded pizzas. They operate a massive production facility in Milwaukee with high-speed automated lines.

## Pain Points
- Production line efficiency across dozens of pizza varieties and sizes
- Ingredient inventory management with fluctuating commodity prices
- Quality consistency across millions of units per week
- Labor scheduling for 24/7 production operations

## Proposed AI Solution
**AI-Driven Production & Inventory Optimization**
- Real-time production line monitoring with anomaly detection for quality issues
- Commodity price forecasting to optimize purchasing timing for cheese, flour, toppings
- Computer vision for pizza topping distribution quality checks
- Intelligent shift scheduling based on demand forecasts and employee preferences

## Estimated Impact
- 5-10% reduction in ingredient costs through smarter purchasing
- 20% fewer quality defects caught earlier in the production process
- Improved employee retention through better scheduling
