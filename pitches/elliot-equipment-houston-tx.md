## AI Integration Consulting Pitch

**Industry:** Manufacturing (Cranes & Heavy Equipment)
**Location:** Houston, TX (Metro)
**Estimated Size:** 200-500 employees

## Pain Points
- Manual scheduling of crane maintenance and inspections across large fleet
- Paper-based field service reports from technicians
- Inventory forecasting for replacement parts is reactive, not predictive

## Proposed AI Solution
- **Predictive Maintenance System**: ML model trained on equipment sensor data and service history to predict failures before they happen, reducing downtime by 20-30%
- **Automated Field Service Reports**: Mobile app with voice-to-text and AI summarization for technician reports
- **Parts Inventory Optimization**: Demand forecasting model to optimize parts stocking levels

## Value Proposition
Reduce unplanned equipment downtime, cut parts inventory costs by 15-20%, and free up 10+ hours/week of administrative work from field technicians.
