# Cascade Dairy Farms — Boise, ID

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Agriculture / Dairy
- **Location:** Boise, Idaho
- **Estimated Size:** 60-80 employees, 2,000+ head herd
- **Type:** Large-scale dairy operation with processing facility

## Pain Points
- Herd health monitoring relies on visual observation
- Feed mix ratios adjusted manually based on gut feel
- Milk yield tracking done in spreadsheets
- Labor scheduling is ad-hoc, leading to overtime costs

## Proposed AI Solution
IoT + AI herd health monitoring that flags early signs of illness from activity and feeding patterns. Feed optimization AI that adjusts rations based on milk output, weather, and market prices. Automated production dashboards replacing manual spreadsheet tracking.

## Value Proposition
- Early illness detection reduces vet costs by 20-25%
- Optimized feed ratios improve milk yield per cow by 5-8%
- Real-time dashboards save 15+ hours/week of manual data entry
