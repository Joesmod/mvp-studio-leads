# Piedmont Healthcare — Atlanta, GA

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Healthcare Administration
- **Location:** Atlanta, Georgia
- **Estimated Size:** 23,000+ employees, 25 hospitals across Georgia
- **Website:** piedmont.org

## AI Use Case: Administrative Workflow Automation & Patient Flow Optimization
Piedmont is one of Georgia's largest health systems. The administrative burden — scheduling, billing, prior authorizations — consumes enormous staff hours.

## Pain Points
- Prior authorization processing taking days of manual back-and-forth
- Patient scheduling inefficiencies leading to no-shows and gaps
- Billing and coding errors causing claim denials and revenue leakage
- Nurse staffing allocation across 25 hospitals based on outdated models

## Proposed Solution
- AI-automated prior authorization with payer rule matching and auto-submission
- Predictive no-show modeling with automated overbooking and reminder optimization
- NLP-based coding assistance to reduce claim denial rates
- Demand-based nurse staffing forecasts using patient census predictions

## Estimated Impact
- 40-60% reduction in prior auth processing time
- 10-15% reduction in patient no-show rates
- Fewer claim denials through automated coding validation
