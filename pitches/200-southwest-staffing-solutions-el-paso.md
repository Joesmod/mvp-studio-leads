# Southwest Staffing Solutions — El Paso, TX

## Company Info
- **Industry:** Professional Services (Staffing/Temp Agency)
- **Location:** El Paso, TX
- **Estimated Size:** 50–80 employees (placing 500+ temps weekly)
- **Type:** Light industrial and warehouse temp staffing

## Pain Points
- Matching workers to jobs is manual — coordinators spend hours on phone calls each morning
- No-show rates average 15-20%, causing client penalties and scrambles for replacements
- Resume/application processing is slow — 30+ minutes per candidate
- Client billing and worker payroll reconciliation has frequent errors

## Proposed AI Solution
- **AI worker-job matching** — auto-matches available workers to open orders by skill, location, reliability score, and preference
- **No-show prediction** — ML model flags high-risk assignments and auto-lines up backups
- **Automated candidate processing** — AI extracts skills, experience, and certifications from applications in seconds
- **Smart billing reconciliation** — AI matches timesheets to invoices, flags discrepancies before they become disputes

## Why Now
Staffing agencies live on speed and reliability. AI matching and no-show prediction directly improve fill rates and client satisfaction.
