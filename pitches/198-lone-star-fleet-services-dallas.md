# Lone Star Fleet Services — Dallas, TX

## Company Info
- **Industry:** Logistics (Fleet Management)
- **Location:** Dallas, TX
- **Estimated Size:** 80–150 employees
- **Type:** Commercial fleet maintenance and management

## Pain Points
- Reactive maintenance causes unexpected breakdowns costing $500-2,000 per roadside event
- Parts inventory is either overstocked (dead capital) or understocked (downtime)
- Driver behavior tracking is limited to basic telematics, not actionable insights
- DOT compliance documentation is manual and audit-stressful

## Proposed AI Solution
- **Predictive maintenance** — ML models on engine/sensor data predict failures 2-4 weeks before breakdown
- **Smart parts inventory** — AI forecasts parts needs based on fleet age, mileage, and failure patterns
- **Driver coaching AI** — analyzes driving patterns and generates personalized coaching to reduce fuel/wear costs
- **Automated DOT compliance** — AI tracks inspection schedules, license renewals, and generates audit-ready reports

## Why Now
Dallas is a logistics hub. Fleet downtime directly impacts revenue. Predictive maintenance alone typically delivers 3-5x ROI.
