# Heartland Ag Services — Kansas City, MO

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Agriculture / Grain Elevator & Farm Services
- **Location:** Kansas City, Missouri
- **Estimated Size:** 100-250 employees
- **Type:** Regional grain handling, storage, and farm input supplier

## Pain Points
- Grain grading and quality assessment is manual and subjective — inconsistent results across locations
- Farmers call in for pricing and availability; phone lines are jammed during harvest season
- Fertilizer and seed inventory management is reactive, not predictive
- Equipment maintenance on elevators and conveyors is break-fix, causing costly downtime during peak

## Proposed AI Solution
- **AI-powered grain grading** — computer vision system for consistent, instant quality assessment at intake
- **Self-service pricing portal with chatbot** — farmers check live prices, place orders, and get delivery ETAs without calling
- **Predictive inventory for farm inputs** — forecast fertilizer/seed demand by region, crop type, and planting calendar
- **Predictive maintenance** on elevator motors, conveyors, and dryers using vibration/temp sensors + ML

## Estimated ROI
- 50% reduction in phone volume during harvest
- Equipment downtime reduction: 30-40%
- Grading consistency improvement: eliminate human variance

## Contact Strategy
- Target: General Manager or Operations Director
- Angle: "Harvest season shouldn't mean chaos — AI can handle the volume so your team focuses on relationships"
