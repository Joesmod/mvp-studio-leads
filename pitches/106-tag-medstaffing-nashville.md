# TAG MedStaffing
## Healthcare Staffing | Nashville, TN

**Industry:** Healthcare Administration / Staffing
**Location:** Nashville, Tennessee
**Estimated Size:** 50-150 employees
**Website:** tagmedstaffing.com

## Pain Points
- Matching healthcare professionals to facility needs is time-intensive and relies on recruiter memory
- Credential verification and compliance tracking across hundreds of placements is manual
- Client facilities submit staffing requests via email/phone with no standardized intake
- Candidate sourcing and screening consumes 60%+ of recruiter time

## Proposed AI Solution
- **AI Matching Engine:** Intelligent matching of healthcare professionals to facility needs based on credentials, location, availability, specialty, and past placement success
- **Automated Credential Tracking:** AI system that monitors license expirations, continuing education requirements, and compliance deadlines with auto-alerts
- **Smart Intake Portal:** AI-powered request processing that standardizes facility needs, auto-matches candidates, and presents options within minutes
- **Candidate Screening Automation:** AI resume parsing, phone screen scheduling, and initial qualification assessment to free recruiters for relationship-building

## Value Proposition
In healthcare staffing, speed to fill is everything. AI matching and automated screening can cut time-to-fill by 40-60%, improving fill rates and client retention while letting recruiters focus on the human side of the business.
