## AI Use Case: Cold Chain Optimization & Production Planning

### Pain Points
- Cold chain monitoring across hundreds of delivery routes relies on manual temperature logging and reactive alerts
- Production planning for perishable dairy products requires balancing shelf life, demand forecasts, and plant capacity
- Retail promotional demand spikes cause stockouts or overproduction
- Fleet maintenance for refrigerated trucks is scheduled by mileage, not actual condition

### Proposed Solution
1. **AI Cold Chain Monitor** — IoT + AI system that predicts temperature excursions before they happen and auto-reroutes at-risk loads
2. **Demand-Driven Production Planner** — ML model incorporating retailer POS data, promotional calendars, and seasonality to optimize plant runs
3. **Promotional Demand Forecaster** — Predict volume spikes from retail promotions with 2-week lead time for production planning
4. **Predictive Fleet Maintenance** — Sensor-based monitoring of refrigeration units and engines to schedule maintenance before breakdowns

### Why Now
Dairy margins depend on minimizing waste and maintaining cold chain integrity. AI turns reactive operations into predictive ones.

### Estimated Value
- 20-30% reduction in spoilage/waste
- 15% improvement in production planning accuracy
- ROI within 5-8 months
