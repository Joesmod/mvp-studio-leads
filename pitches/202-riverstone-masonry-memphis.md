# Riverstone Masonry — Memphis, TN

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Construction / Masonry
- **Location:** Memphis, Tennessee
- **Estimated Size:** 80-120 employees
- **Type:** Commercial masonry and concrete contractor

## Pain Points
- Estimating jobs requires experienced estimators spending days on manual takeoffs
- Material waste from over-ordering (no data-driven forecasting)
- Scheduling crews across multiple job sites is chaotic
- Safety incident tracking is paper-based

## Proposed AI Solution
AI-assisted estimating tool that analyzes blueprints and historical job data to generate accurate material takeoffs in hours instead of days. Crew scheduling optimization based on skill sets, certifications, and project timelines. Digital safety reporting with trend analysis.

## Value Proposition
- Cut estimating time by 60%, bid on more projects
- Reduce material waste by 10-15% through predictive ordering
- Centralized crew scheduling eliminates conflicts and idle time
