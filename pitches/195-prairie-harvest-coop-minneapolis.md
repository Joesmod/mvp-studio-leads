# Prairie Harvest Co-op — Minneapolis, MN

## Company Info
- **Industry:** Agriculture (Grain Co-op)
- **Location:** Minneapolis, MN
- **Estimated Size:** 100–180 employees
- **Type:** Regional grain elevator and farm supply cooperative

## Pain Points
- Grain merchandising decisions rely on manual market tracking and experience
- Harvest logistics (truck scheduling, bin allocation) create bottlenecks during peak season
- Soil testing and agronomic recommendations are slow to turn around
- Member communication about pricing and programs is inconsistent

## Proposed AI Solution
- **AI grain trading assistant** — real-time basis analysis, futures correlation, and sell/hold recommendations
- **Harvest logistics optimizer** — predicts delivery volumes by field/member and auto-schedules receiving
- **Precision ag analytics** — AI interprets soil/yield data to generate field-specific input recommendations
- **Automated member comms** — AI generates personalized market updates and program reminders

## Why Now
Ag margins are tight. Co-ops that leverage data for better merchandising and logistics outperform peers by 10-15% on grain margins.
