# West Logistics Group
## Third-Party Logistics | Atlanta, GA

**Industry:** Logistics / Warehousing
**Location:** Atlanta, Georgia (facilities in Atlanta & Tampa)
**Estimated Size:** 50-200 employees
**Website:** westlogisticsgroup.com

## Pain Points
- Warehouse space allocation is managed manually, leading to underutilized capacity
- Shipment tracking across multiple carriers requires constant manual monitoring
- Customer inquiries about order status consume significant staff time
- Seasonal demand forecasting relies on gut feel rather than data

## Proposed AI Solution
- **Smart Warehouse Allocation:** AI-driven space optimization that automatically assigns storage based on SKU velocity, size, and shipping patterns
- **Carrier Performance Analytics:** Automated tracking and scoring of carrier reliability, cost, and transit times to optimize routing decisions
- **Customer Status Bot:** AI chatbot for real-time order/shipment status that handles 80%+ of routine inquiries
- **Demand Forecasting:** ML-based seasonal and trend analysis to proactively adjust staffing and capacity
