## Proposed AI Solution
- **Unified Fulfillment Intelligence:** AI-driven workflow optimization that dynamically adjusts picking, packing, and shipping processes based on channel requirements and volume

- **Automated Client Onboarding:** AI-assisted integration setup that maps client data formats, suggests workflow configurations, and auto-generates SOPs

## Value Proposition
3PL growth depends on operational efficiency and fast client onboarding. AI-optimized fulfillment can handle 20-30% more volume with the same headcount, while cutting onboarding time from weeks to days improves client acquisition speed.
