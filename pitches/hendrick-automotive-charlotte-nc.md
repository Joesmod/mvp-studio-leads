# Hendrick Automotive Group — Charlotte, NC

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Automotive Dealerships
- **Location:** Charlotte, North Carolina
- **Estimated Size:** 10,000+ employees, 90+ dealership locations across 13 states
- **Website:** hendrickcars.com

## AI Use Case: Multi-Location Inventory Intelligence & Lead Scoring
Hendrick is one of the largest privately held dealership groups in the US. Managing inventory and leads across 90+ locations requires smarter systems.

## Pain Points
- Vehicle inventory imbalances — popular models stuck at low-demand locations
- Internet leads distributed without intelligent scoring or routing
- Service department capacity underutilized at some locations, overbooked at others
- Pricing decisions based on manager intuition rather than market data

## Proposed Solution
- AI inventory transfer recommendations based on local demand signals and days-on-lot
- Lead scoring model prioritizing high-intent buyers and routing to best-fit dealership
- Service capacity balancing with predictive appointment demand modeling
- Competitive pricing intelligence using real-time market comparables

## Estimated Impact
- Faster inventory turns through smart rebalancing
- 20-30% improvement in lead conversion through better scoring
- Higher service department utilization across the group
