# Iron Range Fabrication — Duluth, MN

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Manufacturing / Metal Fabrication
- **Location:** Duluth, Minnesota
- **Estimated Size:** 70-100 employees
- **Type:** Custom steel and aluminum fabrication for mining and industrial equipment

## Pain Points
- Quoting custom jobs takes 3-5 days of engineering time
- Quality control is visual inspection only — defects caught late
- Machine utilization tracking is manual, no OEE visibility
- Skilled welder shortage makes workforce planning critical

## Proposed AI Solution
AI quoting assistant that analyzes CAD files and historical job data to generate accurate quotes in hours. Computer vision quality inspection at key fabrication stages catching defects early. Real-time OEE dashboards from machine sensors showing utilization, downtime causes, and bottlenecks.

## Value Proposition
- Quote turnaround from 5 days to same-day, win more bids
- Catch 90%+ of defects before final assembly
- 10-15% improvement in machine utilization through data visibility
