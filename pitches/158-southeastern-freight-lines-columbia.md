## AI Use Case: Load Optimization & Dock Scheduling

### Pain Points
- LTL trailer loading is partially manual, leading to suboptimal cube utilization
- Dock scheduling at terminals causes driver wait times and congestion
- Claims processing for damaged freight is paper-heavy and slow
- Linehaul routing doesn't dynamically adapt to volume fluctuations

### Proposed Solution
AI-powered 3D load planning that maximizes trailer cube utilization while respecting delivery sequence constraints. Smart dock scheduling system that predicts arrival times and pre-assigns doors. Automated claims triage using shipment data and photo documentation. Dynamic linehaul routing that adjusts nightly based on actual freight volumes.

### Expected Impact
- 8-12% improvement in trailer utilization
- 25% reduction in driver dock wait times
- 40% faster claims resolution
