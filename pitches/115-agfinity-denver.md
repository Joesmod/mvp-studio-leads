# Agfinity Inc. — Denver/Eaton, CO

## Company Info
- **Industry:** Agriculture Supply & Cooperative
- **Location:** Eaton, Colorado (near Denver)
- **Est. Size:** 200-400 employees
- **Type:** Farmer-owned cooperative — energy, agronomy, feed, grain

## AI Use Case: Precision Agronomy Recommendations & Inventory Management

### Pain Points
- Agronomists manually plan crop input recommendations for hundreds of grower accounts
- Seasonal inventory spikes (seed, fertilizer, chemicals) are hard to predict — over-ordering ties up capital, under-ordering loses sales
- Grain marketing decisions rely on manual price tracking and gut feel
- Customer service is overwhelmed during planting and harvest seasons

### Proposed Solution
1. **AI Agronomy Advisor** — Generate field-specific input recommendations using soil data, weather forecasts, and historical yield data
2. **Seasonal Demand Forecaster** — Predict product demand by region and timeframe to optimize purchasing and warehouse allocation
3. **Grain Market Intelligence** — Automated price monitoring with AI-generated sell/hold recommendations for grower members
4. **Seasonal Chatbot** — AI-powered customer service bot to handle routine questions (product availability, delivery status, account balances) during peak seasons

### Why Now
Ag cooperatives are competing with tech-forward agribusiness giants. AI levels the playing field for regional co-ops.

### Estimated Value
- 15-20% improvement in input recommendation accuracy
- 20% reduction in seasonal inventory overstock
- ROI within one growing season (6-9 months)
