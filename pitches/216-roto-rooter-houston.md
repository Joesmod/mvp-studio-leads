# Roto-Rooter Services — Houston, TX

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Professional Services (Plumbing/Drain)
- **Location:** Houston, TX (regional franchise operations)
- **Estimated Size:** 200-500 employees (Houston metro)
- **Website:** rotorooter.com

## AI Opportunity
**Use Case:** AI dispatch optimization and predictive service routing

**Pain Points:**
- Emergency calls require rapid technician dispatch — current routing is manual and slow
- No way to predict seasonal demand spikes (e.g., freeze events, heavy rain flooding)
- Customer follow-up and recurring service reminders are inconsistent

**Proposed Solution:**
- AI-powered smart dispatch that matches technician skills, location, and availability to incoming calls in real-time
- Weather-correlated demand prediction to pre-position crews before storm events
- Automated customer lifecycle management — reminders for annual drain cleaning, water heater checks
- AI call analysis to identify upsell opportunities and improve first-call resolution rates
