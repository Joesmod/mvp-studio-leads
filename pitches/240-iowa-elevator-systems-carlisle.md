# Iowa Elevator Systems & Service Inc. — Carlisle, IA

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Agriculture / Grain Elevator Services
- **Location:** Carlisle, Iowa (serving central Iowa region)
- **Estimated Size:** 20-75 employees
- **Website:** iowaelevatorsystems.com

## AI Opportunity
**Service scheduling optimization and equipment lifecycle management**

## Pain Points
- Scheduling millwright crews across multiple grain elevator client sites
- 40-ton crane and heavy equipment utilization tracking
- Seasonal demand spikes during harvest create scheduling bottlenecks
- Equipment repair vs. replace decisions made without data-driven analysis

## Proposed Solution
AI-powered field service management that:
- Optimizes crew dispatch and routing based on job priority, location, and skill requirements
- Predicts equipment maintenance needs based on usage patterns and age
- Automates scheduling during peak harvest season to maximize crew utilization
- Provides data-driven repair vs. upgrade recommendations for client equipment

## Pitch Angle
"A specialized grain elevator service company with certified millwrights and heavy equipment — AI scheduling could maximize crew utilization during the critical harvest season when every hour of downtime costs farmers money."
