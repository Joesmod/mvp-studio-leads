## AI Opportunity
**Inventory & Pricing Optimization**

### Pain Points
- Managing inventory across 8 dealership locations with multiple brands (Ford, Toyota, Hyundai, Kia, Mazda, etc.)

- Customer follow-up and lead nurturing is inconsistent across locations

### Proposed Solution
AI-powered inventory management system that analyzes regional demand patterns, competitor pricing, and seasonal trends to optimize stock allocation across all 8 locations. Add automated lead scoring and follow-up sequencing to ensure no prospect falls through the cracks. Service department AI scheduling to maximize technician utilization and reduce customer wait times.

- 15-20% improvement in inventory turn rate
- 25% increase in lead-to-sale conversion through automated follow-up
- 30% reduction in service department idle time
