# Ben E. Keith Company — Dallas/Fort Worth, TX

## Company Info
- **Industry:** Food & Beverage Distribution
- **Location:** Fort Worth, Texas (HQ) — distribution across Southern US
- **Est. Size:** 5,000+ employees
- **Founded:** 1906
- **Type:** Privately held, one of the largest independent food distributors in the US

## AI Use Case: Route Optimization & Demand Forecasting

### Pain Points
- Delivery route planning is semi-manual, leading to excess fuel costs and missed time windows
- Demand forecasting for perishable goods relies heavily on rep intuition — overstock means waste, understock means lost sales
- Sales reps manage hundreds of accounts with minimal data on reorder patterns
- Warehouse picking efficiency varies widely across shifts

### Proposed Solution
1. **AI Route Optimizer** — Dynamic daily route planning factoring in traffic, delivery windows, truck capacity, and order priority
2. **Perishable Demand Forecaster** — ML model predicting order volumes by customer, product, and day of week to reduce spoilage and stockouts
3. **Smart Sales Copilot** — AI assistant for reps that surfaces reorder suggestions, cross-sell opportunities, and at-risk accounts
4. **Warehouse Pick Optimization** — Intelligent pick path routing and wave planning to reduce labor per order

### Why Now
Food distribution margins are razor-thin. Every percentage point saved in logistics or waste goes straight to the bottom line.

### Estimated Value
- 10-20% reduction in delivery fuel/labor costs
- 25% reduction in perishable waste
- ROI within 4-7 months
