## AI Use Case
**AI Candidate Matching & Shift Filling**

## Pain Points
- Matching candidates to open shifts is manual and time-consuming
- No-show rates are high (15-20%) with no automated backfill
- Recruiters spend most time on admin (calling, texting, scheduling) vs. relationship building

## Proposed Solution
AI matching engine that scores candidates against job requirements, availability, location, and reliability history. Automated no-show detection with instant backfill outreach via text/call. AI recruiter assistant handling scheduling, onboarding paperwork, and compliance checks. Target: 50% faster fill times, no-show rate under 8%.
