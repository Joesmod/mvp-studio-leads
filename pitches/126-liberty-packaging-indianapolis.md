# Liberty Packaging Solutions — Indianapolis, IN

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Manufacturing / Corrugated Packaging
- **Location:** Indianapolis, Indiana
- **Estimated Size:** 150-300 employees
- **Type:** Custom corrugated packaging manufacturer

## Pain Points
- Die setup and changeover times eat into production — 15-20% of shift time lost
- Quality defects caught late in the line; manual visual inspection misses flaws
- Order entry from customer specs is manual — transcription errors cause costly reprints
- Raw material (linerboard) purchasing is reactive, not optimized against price trends

## Proposed AI Solution
- **Changeover optimization** — AI sequences production orders to minimize die changes and setup time
- **Computer vision QC** — inline cameras detect print defects, crush damage, and dimensional errors in real-time
- **Automated order processing** — AI reads customer specs/drawings and auto-populates order system, flags ambiguities for review
- **Material price forecasting** — ML model tracks linerboard commodity trends and recommends optimal purchase timing

## Estimated ROI
- Setup time reduction: 25-35%
- Defect rate reduction: 50%+
- Order entry errors: near-zero
- Raw material savings: 5-8% annually

## Contact Strategy
- Target: Plant Manager or VP Manufacturing
- Angle: "Every setup minute is money — AI can sequence your runs smarter and catch defects your eyes miss"
