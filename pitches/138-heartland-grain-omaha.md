# Heartland Grain & Feed — Omaha, NE

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Agriculture / Grain Trading & Feed Manufacturing
- **Location:** Omaha, NE
- **Estimated Size:** 150–300 employees
- **Operations:** 12 grain elevators, 2 feed mills across Nebraska and Iowa

## Pain Points
- Grain purchasing decisions based on gut feel and delayed market data
- Feed mill production scheduling done weekly on spreadsheets — can't adapt to demand shifts
- Truck logistics for grain hauling coordinated by phone with independent drivers
- Compliance documentation (FSMA, FDA) assembled manually for audits

## Proposed AI Solution
- **Market intelligence dashboard:** AI aggregates futures, basis, weather, and USDA reports to recommend optimal buy/sell timing
- **Dynamic production scheduling:** AI adjusts feed mill runs based on real-time orders, ingredient availability, and equipment status
- **Logistics coordination platform:** AI-matched loads to available trucks, optimizing routes and reducing empty miles
- **Compliance automation:** AI maintains audit-ready documentation, auto-generating reports from production and sourcing data

## Value Proposition
Improve grain trading margins by 5-8%, reduce feed mill changeover waste by 25%, and cut logistics costs by 15%.
