# Magnolia Auto Group — Nashville, TN

## Company Info
- **Industry:** Auto Dealers
- **Location:** Nashville, TN
- **Estimated Size:** 200–350 employees across 5 dealerships
- **Type:** Multi-brand auto dealership group

## Pain Points
- Lead follow-up is inconsistent — internet leads go cold within hours
- Service department scheduling is inefficient, averaging 65% bay utilization
- Used car pricing relies on gut feel, leading to aged inventory
- Customer retention after warranty expiration drops sharply

## Proposed AI Solution
- **AI lead response** — instant personalized follow-up to web/phone leads within 60 seconds, 24/7
- **Service bay optimization** — ML-based scheduling that accounts for job complexity, tech skill, and parts availability
- **Dynamic used car pricing** — AI analyzes market comps, days-on-lot trends, and local demand to recommend pricing adjustments
- **Predictive retention outreach** — identifies customers likely to defect and triggers targeted service offers

## Why Now
Nashville is one of the fastest-growing auto markets. Dealers who respond fastest and price smartest capture disproportionate share.
