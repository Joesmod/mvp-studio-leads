## AI Use Case

## Pain Points
- Dispatching concrete trucks manually leads to missed delivery windows and wasted loads
- Drivers idle while dispatchers juggle phone calls, weather delays, and job site readiness
- Concrete has a short usable window (~90 min) making timing critical

## Proposed Solution
AI-powered dispatch system that optimizes truck routing based on real-time traffic, job site readiness confirmations, and batch plant capacity. Predictive scheduling that accounts for weather, cure times, and historical pour patterns. Target: 20% fewer wasted loads, 30% reduction in driver idle time.
