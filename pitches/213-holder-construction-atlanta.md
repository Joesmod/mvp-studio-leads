## AI Opportunity
**Use Case:** AI-powered project estimation and safety monitoring

**Pain Points:**
- Project cost estimation relies heavily on senior estimators' tribal knowledge
- Safety incident tracking is reactive rather than predictive
- Document management across large commercial projects is chaotic (RFIs, submittals, change orders)

**Proposed Solution:**
- AI estimating assistant trained on historical project data to generate faster, more accurate bids
- Computer vision on jobsite cameras for real-time safety violation detection (missing PPE, exclusion zones)
- Intelligent document routing that auto-classifies RFIs and submittals, flags conflicts, and suggests responses
- Weather and supply chain AI to proactively adjust project schedules
