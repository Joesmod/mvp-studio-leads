# Harvest Food Distributors — Denver, CO

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Food & Beverage Distribution
- **Location:** Aurora, CO (Denver metro)
- **Estimated Size:** 50-150 employees
- **Website:** harvestfooddistributors.com

## AI Opportunity
**Use Case:** AI route optimization and demand forecasting

**Pain Points:**
- Fleet of 8+ trucks covering CO, WY, UT, KS — route planning is manual and suboptimal
- Perishable inventory means waste from over-ordering or mis-forecasting demand
- Sales team relies on gut feel rather than data for account growth opportunities

**Proposed Solution:**
- AI-powered route optimization reducing fuel costs and delivery times across multi-state territory
- Demand forecasting model trained on seasonal patterns, restaurant ordering history, and local events
- Automated reorder suggestions for customers based on their consumption patterns
- Spoilage prediction to prioritize near-expiry inventory for immediate delivery
