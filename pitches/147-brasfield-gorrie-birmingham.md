# Brasfield & Gorrie
## AI Integration Consulting Pitch

**Industry:** Construction / General Contractor
**Location:** Birmingham, AL
**Estimated Size:** 4,000+ employees, $6B+ revenue
**Website:** brasfieldgorrie.com

## Company Overview
Brasfield & Gorrie is one of the largest privately held general contractors in the US, ranked in the ENR Top 20. They build healthcare facilities, commercial buildings, industrial plants, and multifamily across the Southeast. Headquartered in Birmingham with offices across 10+ states.

## Pain Points
- Subcontractor prequalification and performance tracking across thousands of subs
- Change order management and cost tracking on large projects
- Document management across complex multi-phase projects
- Workforce planning for seasonal and project-based labor needs

## Proposed AI Solution
**AI-Enhanced Subcontractor Management & Document Intelligence**
- AI-powered subcontractor scoring using historical performance, safety records, and financial health
- Automated change order analysis — flag cost impacts and compare against historical baselines
- Intelligent document search and extraction across project files (RFIs, submittals, specs)
- Predictive workforce planning based on project pipeline and historical staffing patterns

## Estimated Impact
- 20-30% faster subcontractor prequalification
- Reduced change order disputes through better documentation analysis
- Hours saved per week in document search and retrieval
- Better labor planning reducing overtime and idle time

## Outreach Strategy
Target VP of Preconstruction or Director of Innovation. Brasfield & Gorrie is large enough to have dedicated innovation resources — position as a competitive differentiator for winning bids.
