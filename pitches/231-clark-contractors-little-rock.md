# Clark Contractors LLC — Little Rock, AR

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Commercial Construction
- **Location:** Little Rock, Arkansas
- **Estimated Size:** 200-500 employees
- **Revenue:** ~$156M (2019)
- **Website:** clarkcontractors.com

## AI Opportunity
**Project scheduling and resource allocation optimization**

## Pain Points
- Complex multi-site project coordination across Arkansas and surrounding states
- Manual scheduling of subcontractors, materials, and equipment across concurrent builds
- Cost overruns from misaligned labor scheduling and material delivery timing
- Safety compliance tracking across dozens of active job sites

## Proposed Solution
AI-powered project management layer that:
- Predicts material delivery delays and auto-adjusts schedules
- Optimizes crew allocation across concurrent projects based on skill requirements and proximity
- Automates safety inspection scheduling and compliance documentation
- Generates real-time cost variance alerts with root cause analysis

## Pitch Angle
"A $156M construction firm managing schools, hospitals, and hotels across multiple states — AI scheduling could save 10-15% on labor costs alone by eliminating crew idle time and material wait delays."
