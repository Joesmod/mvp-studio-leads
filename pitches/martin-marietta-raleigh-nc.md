## AI Use Case: Predictive Maintenance & Fleet Optimization
Martin Marietta operates hundreds of quarries, plants, and a massive fleet of heavy equipment. Downtime on crushers, conveyors, and haul trucks costs millions annually.

## Pain Points
- Unplanned equipment failures causing production delays
- Manual inspection schedules that miss early warning signs
- Fleet routing inefficiencies across distributed quarry sites
- Inventory forecasting for aggregates across regional markets

## Proposed Solution
- IoT sensor integration with AI-driven predictive maintenance models for crushers and conveyors
- Fleet route optimization using real-time demand and traffic data
- Demand forecasting for aggregate products by region and season
- Automated quality control using computer vision at plant output

## Estimated Impact
- 20-30% reduction in unplanned downtime
- 10-15% fleet fuel savings through optimized routing
- Faster response to regional demand shifts
