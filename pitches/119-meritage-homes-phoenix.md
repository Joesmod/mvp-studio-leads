# Meritage Homes — Scottsdale, AZ

## Company Info
- **Industry:** Residential Construction / Homebuilding
- **Location:** Scottsdale, Arizona (HQ) — builds across 9 states
- **Est. Size:** 4,000+ employees
- **Founded:** 1985
- **Type:** Public (NYSE: MTH) — top 5 US homebuilder by volume

## AI Use Case: Sales Pipeline Optimization & Construction Schedule Management

### Pain Points
- Online lead volume is massive but qualification is manual — sales teams chase low-intent prospects
- Construction scheduling across hundreds of active homes relies on manual coordination with subcontractors
- Warranty claim tracking and resolution is fragmented — homeowners complain about response times
- Material procurement across communities doesn't leverage aggregate purchasing power intelligently

### Proposed Solution
1. **AI Lead Scoring** — Score and prioritize online leads based on browsing behavior, community interest, and demographic signals
2. **Construction Schedule AI** — Predict sub delays, auto-reschedule dependent tasks, and alert supers to at-risk closings
3. **Warranty Chatbot + Triage** — AI-powered homeowner portal that handles routine warranty requests and routes complex issues to the right trade
4. **Procurement Optimizer** — Aggregate demand forecasting across communities to negotiate better pricing and reduce material delays

### Why Now
Homebuilders are volume businesses — small efficiency gains across thousands of homes compound into massive savings.

### Estimated Value
- 25% improvement in lead-to-contract conversion
- 10-15% reduction in construction cycle time delays
- ROI within 4-8 months
