# Prestige Auto Group — Tampa, FL

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Automotive Dealership
- **Location:** Tampa, Florida
- **Estimated Size:** 150-300 employees across multiple locations
- **Type:** Multi-brand auto dealership group

## Pain Points
- Lead follow-up is inconsistent — internet leads go stale within hours
- Service department scheduling is phone-heavy; no-shows waste technician time
- Used car pricing relies on manager gut feel rather than real-time market data
- F&I (Finance & Insurance) process is slow — customers wait 45-90 min for paperwork

## Proposed AI Solution
- **AI lead scoring and auto-follow-up** — instant text/email response to web leads, prioritize by purchase likelihood
- **Smart service scheduling** — AI books appointments, sends reminders, fills cancellation slots automatically
- **Dynamic used car pricing** — ML model ingests auction data, local comps, and days-on-lot to recommend optimal pricing
- **F&I document automation** — pre-fill forms, suggest products based on customer profile, cut wait time by 60%

## Estimated ROI
- Lead conversion improvement: 20-35%
- Service no-show reduction: 40%
- Used car turn rate improvement: 15-20%
- F&I time per deal: from 75 min to 30 min

## Contact Strategy
- Target: Dealer Principal or General Manager
- Angle: "Every hour a lead sits untouched costs you $500. Let AI work your leads while your team works the floor"
