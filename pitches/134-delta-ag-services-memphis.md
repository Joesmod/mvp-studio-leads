# Delta Agricultural Services — Memphis, TN

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Agriculture / Crop Services
- **Location:** Memphis, TN (serves Mid-South region)
- **Estimated Size:** 75–150 employees
- **Website:** deltaagservices.com

## Pain Points
- Crop scouting reports are handwritten and filed days after field visits
- Fertilizer and chemical application rates set by rule-of-thumb, not soil data analysis
- Equipment utilization across multiple farm clients is poorly tracked
- Seasonal labor scheduling is chaotic — over-staffed some weeks, short-handed others

## Proposed AI Solution
- **Digital scouting with AI analysis:** Mobile app captures field photos; AI identifies pest/disease issues and generates instant recommendations
- **Precision application modeling:** Analyze soil tests, weather, and yield history to optimize input rates per field zone, reducing chemical waste
- **Equipment utilization dashboard:** GPS and usage data analyzed by AI to optimize equipment routing and sharing across client farms
- **Labor demand forecasting:** Predict staffing needs by week based on crop stages, weather forecasts, and historical patterns

## Value Proposition
Reduce input costs by 15%, improve scouting turnaround from days to hours, and optimize labor allocation to cut overtime by 30%.
