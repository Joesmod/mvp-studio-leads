# Earnhardt Auto Centers — Phoenix, AZ

## Company Overview
- **Industry:** Automotive Dealerships
- **Location:** Phoenix, AZ (multiple locations across the Valley)
- **Estimated Size:** 500-1,000 employees
- **Website:** nobull.com

## AI Opportunity
**Use Case:** AI-powered customer engagement and inventory optimization

**Pain Points:**
- Managing inventory across multiple dealership locations manually
- Slow follow-up on online leads and service appointment scheduling
- Difficulty predicting which vehicles will sell fastest at which locations

**Proposed Solution:**
- AI chatbot for 24/7 lead capture and appointment scheduling across all locations
- Predictive analytics for inventory allocation — move the right cars to the right lots
- Automated follow-up sequences for service reminders and trade-in opportunities
- Sentiment analysis on customer reviews to flag service issues early
